Question 2

Making information open and easily accessible to the public in accordance with the Open Government initiatives promotes the Government of Canada's transparency and accountability. True or false?Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

Federal information that is open and easily accessible to all Canadians as part of the Open Government initiatives supports the transparency and accountability of the Government of Canada.

Question 3

Which of the following are benefits of properly managing information?
Select all that apply.

Correct.

Incorrect. The correct answers are A, C, and D.

The benefits of information management include having easy and simple access to the information resources you need, support for project reporting and performance assessment, and transparency in the decision-making process. Properly managing information also means eliminating the duplication of work efforts for employees and a reduction in the storage space required and the associated cost.
