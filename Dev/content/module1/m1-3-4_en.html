Get Involved

You can support open government by following IM best practices and guidelines which help facilitate the effective identification, management and release of federal information resources to the Canadian public through the Open Government Portal.


Did you know?

The portal provides the public with one‑stop access to the GC's searchable open information resources. This not only includes machine-readable data, but also mandatory departmental reports such as Access to Information summaries and proactive disclosure reports. The information resources published on the portal is made available by federal departments, agencies, and Crown corporations.

 

If you are interested in becoming more involved in open government, consider connecting with:


Coordinator

Reach out to your open government coordinator to learn more about available opportunities. Most organizations have designated open government coordinators.


Community

Connect with others in the open government community through GCpedia and GCconnex.


TBS

Contact the open government team at Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat at open-ouvert@tbs-sct.gc.ca for more information on how to get involved in open government implementation.
