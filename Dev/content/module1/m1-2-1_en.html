Information Has Value

Managing information is important because the information you use at work on a regular basis is valuable. It has value for:

You and your colleagues, as timely access to information can affect employee behaviour, decision-making processes, and business outcomes.

Your organization, as it needs information for its day‑to‑day operations and for its corporate memory.

The public, as current and future generations of Canadians require information to benefit from different programs and services.
