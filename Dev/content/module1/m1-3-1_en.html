Information Management Framework

The Government of Canada recognizes that information is a public asset and considers managing information a top priority.

The Government of Canada has an information management framework to ensure that all employees have the appropriate support in the effective and efficient management of information.

The IM framework includes:

  • IM vision
  • IM strategy
  • IM policy instruments

For more information on the Government of Canada IM framework, visit the Government of Canada information management web page.
