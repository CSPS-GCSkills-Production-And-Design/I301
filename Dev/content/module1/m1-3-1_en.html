Service and Digital Policy Framework

The Government of Canada recognizes that information and data are a public asset and considers managing them a top priority.

The Government of Canada has various strategies and a new Service and Digital policy framework to ensure that the GC moves towards being a digital and open government and that all employees have the appropriate tools, skills and support in the effective and efficient management of information and data.

The Service and Digital policy framework includes:

  • IM-IT vision
  • IM-IT, Digital and Data Roadmap strategies
  • Service and Digital policy instruments

For more information on the Government of Canada Service and Digital policy framework, visit the Government of Canada Digital government web page
