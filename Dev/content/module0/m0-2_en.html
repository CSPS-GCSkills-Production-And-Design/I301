What You Will Learn

The course introduces basic information management (IM) concepts and describes your IM roles and responsibilities as an employee of the Government of Canada.

After completing this course, you will be able to:

  • Recognize the benefits of managing information in your daily work;
  • Describe your IM roles and responsibilities;
  • Differentiate information resources of business value from transitory information; and
  • Follow IM best practices for managing electronic information.
