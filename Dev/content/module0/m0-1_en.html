Welcome

Welcome to the "Fundamentals of Information Management" — an online course for all public servants.

Understanding and applying proper information management practices will help you reduce the amount of time you spend looking for the information you need. It will enable you to get the right information to the right people, for the right purpose, and at the right time.

This course was created by the Canada School of Public Service in collaboration with the Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat and information management specialists from other federal departments and agencies.
