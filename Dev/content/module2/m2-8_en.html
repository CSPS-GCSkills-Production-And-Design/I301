Module Summary

In this module, you learned about information resources and corporate repositories. The key points are as follows:

  • The definition of information resources includes any documentary material we create, receive or use, regardless of its source, type or format.
  • Information of business value is used to make decisions, to deliver programs and services, and to report on plans, priorities, and performance.
  • Information not identified as having business value is considered transitory.
  • It is your responsibility to save and organize information resources that have business value and to dispose of or delete transitory information.
  • It is your responsibility to save information of business value in a designated corporate repository.
