Module Summary

In this module, you learned about information resources and corporate repositories. The key points are as follows:

  • The definition of information resources includes any documentary material we create, receive, or use, regardless of its source, type, or format.
  • Information resources of business value are used to make decisions, to deliver programs and services, and to report on plans, priorities, and performance.
  • Information resources not identified as having business value are considered transitory.
  • It is your responsibility to save and organize information resources that have business value and dispose of or delete transitory information.
  • It is your responsibility to save information resources of business value in a designated corporate repository.
