Question 1

Which of the following are considered information resources?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect. The correct answer is E.

The definition of information resources includes all of the ways we create, receive, or use information – regardless of its source, type, or format.

Question 2

Denise receives an email from her client approving the project activity schedule she had proposed. Denise is the person responsible for the project. For Denise, this email is an information resource that has business value. True or false?Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

The key word here is "approval", and for Denise – who is responsible for the project – this email is an information of business value.

Question 3

George receives a phone message from his manager confirming that she has approved his vacation as requested in the official HR system. This phone message is an information of business value. True or false?Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

Since the request was approved in the official system, the phone message is transitory.

Question 4

Marie has saved the final project documents in GCdocs – the approved corporate repository of her organization. Marie has also saved a copy of the same documents on a USB key for future reference. The documents on the USB key are information of business value. True or false?Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

The key word here is "copy". Since the final project documents are filed in the approved corporate repository, the USB copy is transitory.

Question 5

You are working on an important business document. Where do you need to save your final version?

Correct.
