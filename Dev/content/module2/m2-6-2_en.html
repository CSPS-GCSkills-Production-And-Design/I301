Examples of Corporate Repositories

There are different systems that can serve as repositories. Depending on your organization, a corporate repository might be:

  • GCDOCS, or any other electronic document and records management system;
  • Designated shared drives; or
  • Physical space, such as a records centre.

Check with your manager or IM specialist about where you should save your information. Your organization has guidelines in place that will help you identify where and how your information resources should be saved and what additional information is needed when saving them.

Remember that your desktop, Outlook, an unmanaged shared drive, or a Wiki are not considered corporate repositories.
