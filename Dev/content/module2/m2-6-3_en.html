Duty to Document

It is your responsibility as an employee of the Government of Canada to document your activities and decisions.

You should follow your organization's guidelines on how to use the official corporate repository and how to save information resources. You should always follow the naming conventions of your organization and capture metadata if required.

Metadata (Data that defines and describes other data and is used to aid the identification, description, location, or use of information systems, resources, and elements) is information or details about information resources that you are creating. When you capture metadata, you are building the context for an information resource to be searched and reused. Your information resource metadata could provide details on:
