Social Media for Work and Personal Use

While social media can be used for professional purposes, it is primarily used for personal reasons.

Did you know?

We Canadians are big social media users. According to the statistics portal Statista, the number of social media users in Canada in 2015 exceeded 18 million.

Do you also use social media on public platforms such as Twitter, Facebook, or LinkedIn for personal reasons?

There are rules and guidelines to follow when using social media for work or personal reasons. Get to know the guidelines established by your organization and always adhere to the Values and Ethics Code for the Public Sector.

Check out the learning resources available at CSPS, such as the video Social Media at Work, to learn more about using social media as part of your work or professional interests.
