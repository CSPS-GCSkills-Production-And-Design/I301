Module Summary

In this module, you learned why and how you need to manage your electronic messages. The key points are as follows:

  • It is your responsibility to manage all of your electronic messaging, just as you would any other information resources;
  • You must treat messages as government property and use a standardized bilingual email signature block in all emails you send;
  • You are responsible for saving electronic messages of business value in the designated corporate repository;
  • You should regularly delete any transitory electronic messages that are no longer required; and
  • When using social media for work or personal purposes, you are expected to follow the rules and guidelines established by your organization.
