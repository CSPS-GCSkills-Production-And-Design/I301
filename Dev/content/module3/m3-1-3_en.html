It Starts with You!

The process of managing email begins with you – at your own desktop – when you make decisions about which emails and instant messages to delete, which ones to keep, and where to file them.

As the Government of Canada is constantly evolving, some of the government-wide initiatives and projects may affect your information management practices. The email platform @canada.ca is one example. When this Email Transformation Initiative started, cleaning up emails became a major priority in many organizations.
