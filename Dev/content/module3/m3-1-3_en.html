It Starts with You!

The process of managing email begins with you – at your own desktop – when you make decisions about which emails and instant messages to delete, which ones to keep, and where to file them.

As the Government of Canada is constantly evolving, some of the government-wide initiatives and projects may affect your information management practices. The new email platform @canada.ca is one recent example. When this Email Transformation Initiative started, cleaning up emails became a major priority in many organizations.

Did you know?

The Email Transformation Initiative (ETI) is led by Shared Services Canada (SSC). This single, consolidated system serving the 43 departments and agencies for which SSC provides services replaces the 63 existing email systems in these organizations.
