Information Resources in the Government of Canada

You may use information or data from any source, of any type, or in any format. Depending on your organization, you may work with:

  • Service agreements for specific services in a Word document;
  • Financial records in an Excel spreadsheet format;
  • Electrical plans for an office building in the form of a drawing;
  • Data obtained during an interview with a job candidate that was recorded;
  • Core samples obtained by drilling from the ocean floor, soil, or ice; and
  • Paintings or sculptures that need restoration to preserve their historical and cultural value.
