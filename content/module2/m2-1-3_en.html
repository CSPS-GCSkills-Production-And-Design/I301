Types of Information Resources

The types of information resources depend on their function. As an employee of the Government of Canada, you may write reports, set up meetings, communicate decisions, etc.

Here are examples of types of information resources:

  • memos
  • reports
  • meeting notes
  • invoices
  • contracts
  • database
  • wiki
