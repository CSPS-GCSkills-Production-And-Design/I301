What Is a Corporate Repository?

When you create an information resource that has business value, you need to save it in a place where it can be shared and used by others. That location is your corporate repository.

A repository is a preservation environment for information and data resources which includes specified physical or electronic storage space and the associated infrastructure required for its maintenance.

A corporate repository is the controlled environment for the management of information resources within your organization. It provides sufficient control for the resources to be authentic, reliable, accessible and usable on a continuing basis.
