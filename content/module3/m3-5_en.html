Question 1

Rob receives multiple instant messages concerning a project for which he is responsible. Some messages contain approvals from his manager for decisions Rob has made, and some include casual discussions and meeting reminders. Rob should save all his instant messages in his organization's approved corporate repository. True or false?Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

Rob must save the final emails or instant message conversations that have business value in the appropriate corporate repository. In this case, Rob can delete his instant messages containing casual information as soon as he no longer requires them.

Question 2

Catherine receives information that affects an operational decision on her current project via the message forum on a social media site. Catherine should capture and save this information in her organization's designated corporate repository. True or false?Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

Catherine must document all information of business value, regardless of its origin or format.
