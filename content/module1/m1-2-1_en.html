Information and Data Have Value

Managing information is important because the information and data you use at work on a regular basis is valuable. They have value for:

You and your colleagues, as timely access to information and data can affect employee behaviour, decision-making processes, and business outcomes.

Your organization, as it needs information and data for its day‑to‑day operations and for its corporate memory.

The public, as current and future generations of Canadians require information and data to benefit from different programs and services.
