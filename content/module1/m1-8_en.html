Module Summary

In this module, you learned what information management is and why managing information is important. The key points are as follows:

  • If you create or receive information, you also need to manage it. Managing information is about organizing and using it appropriately.
  • The Government of Canada recognizes that information and data are a public asset and managing information effectively and efficiently is its top priority.
  • Managing information properly has great benefits for you as a government employee, for your organization, and for all Canadians.
  • As a government employee (this includes employees, managers, IM professionals, and IM-IT senior officials), you are responsible for properly managing information.
Now you can proceed to the next module, where you will learn about the different types of information resources.
