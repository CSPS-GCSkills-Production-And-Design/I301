Question 1 of 

How should you name a document when you save it in a corporate repository?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect. The correct answer is D.

When you save and organize information of business value in a corporate repository, you should follow your organization's naming conventions. It will be easy to find and share it when required.

Question 2 of 

According to the Open Government initiatives, all government information and data are open to the public by default without exceptions, thus allowing Canadians to access the information they need. True or false?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

The Government of Canada supports the "open-by-default" culture to ensure that all Canadians have access to the information they need. However, there are exceptions to the open-by-default principle, including personal information, Cabinet confidences, etc.

Question 3 of 

Information that you as a government employee create has value only for the Government of Canada. True or false?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

Information that you as a government employee create has value not only for the Government of Canada, but for all Canadians who benefit from government programs and services.

Question 4 of 

Which of the following statements is characteristic of transitory information?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect. The correct answer is B.

Information resources that are used as drafts or copies for reference purposes or containing information of short-term value are transitory.

Question 5 of 

You are responsible for saving all the information resources you handle in the appropriate corporate repository. True or false?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

Not all information is created equal. You must keep information resources that have business value. Some transitory information could also be kept (for sharing purposes or when starting a draft document) in the corporate repository, but it is your responsibility to delete it once it is no longer required.

Question 6 of 

Brian is a member of a committee that uses an internal wiki to work collaboratively on a project. He is responsible for posting committee meetings' records of decisions. Brian can use the internal wiki and doesn't need to take the additional step of saving the records of decision in GCdocs, which is the designated corporate repository for his organization. True or false?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

Records of decision of meetings are considered information of business value and therefore need to be saved in an appropriate corporate repository, which in this case is GCdocs. An internal wiki is not considered an appropriate corporate repository.

Question 7 of 

Pierre receives an email from his manager asking for his comments on a presentation drafted for an upcoming meeting with internal stakeholders. Pierre replies to the email with his suggestions. For Pierre, this email (a request and a reply) is transitory information. True or false?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

In this context, the manager is responsible for the task, and Pierre was asked for comments only. This email has business value for the manager, but is transitory information for Pierre.

Question 8 of 

Which of the following are considered transitory information and can therefore be deleted?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect. The correct answer is E.

Drafts, notes, and working materials for reference purposes are only used for a limited period of time to ensure the completion of a routine action or the preparation of information of business value. Therefore, they are considered transitory and are not required by your organization.

Question 9 of 

Julie is leaving her current position for a two‑year assignment with another organization. It is the responsibility of an information management professional to clean up, delete, and organize the paper and electronic documents that Julie leaves behind. True or false?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

When employees leave their positions (either temporarily or permanently), it is their responsibility to follow the internal policies for the administrative closure of the business processes, organize and file information of business value accordingly, and properly destroy or delete all transitory information.

Question 10 of 

What determines the value and significance of an information resource?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect. The correct answer is C.

One type, one format, or one source is not necessarily more significant than another. It is the content within an information resource that determines its value and significance.

Question 11 of 

Simon has received an Access to Information request and is searching through relevant documents. He identifies several emails of a transitory nature related to this request that he has kept. Because these email messages contain transitory information, Simon doesn't need to disclose them and can delete them. True or false?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

Records that are subject to an Access to Information request, regardless of their transitory nature, must not be destroyed, modified, or deleted, and must be provided to the requester.

Question 12 of 

Your organization's communication team sends out an email to all employees containing the latest newsletter related to changes in the organizational governance structure. For the communication team, this email is an information of business value. True or false?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

For the communication team, this is an information resource that has business value, because they are responsible for the production and distribution of the newsletter. For you, this is transitory information.

Question 13 of 

Nathalie attends a project team meeting during which she is responsible for note taking. After the meeting, Nathalie transcribes the notes electronically and shares them with her team members and the project lead. The handwritten notes in Nathalie's notebook are transitory information and may be disposed of. True or false?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect.

This is transitory information as Nathalie has already transcribed the notes and shared them.

Question 14 of 

Which of the following information resources might have business value and therefore need to be captured in the corporate repository?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect. The correct answer is D.

It is content, not format, that determines business value. If information, regardless of format, has business value, you need to capture it in your departmental corporate repository.

Question 15 of 

Which of the following is an example of poor email management practice?

Select the correct answer.

Correct.

Incorrect. The correct answer is B.

Proper email management practice is to use links or paths to project files rather than attachments whenever possible.
