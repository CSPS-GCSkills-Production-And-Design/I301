Course Outline

The course consists of three modules and a final quiz.

  • Module 1: The What and Why of Information Management
  • Module 2: Information Resources
  • Module 3: Managing Electronic Messages
  • Final Quiz

In order to successfully complete this course, you will need to score at least 80% on the final quiz. If you don’t get a passing mark, don’t worry! You can retake the quiz as often as required.

It will take you approximately 60 minutes to complete the course.

Good luck!
